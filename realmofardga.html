Project Overview

Game Summary: Realm Of Ardga is a 2D top-down fantasy RPG built in Unity featuring real-time combat, enemy AI, RPG progression, loot collection, and skill-based character development. Players explore a fantasy world while fighting enemies, collecting treasure, and unlocking increasingly powerful abilities.

Core Goal: The project was designed to combine responsive combat with rewarding RPG progression systems. Players become stronger by defeating enemies, earning experience, unlocking skills, and improving their character throughout their adventure.

Development Context: Realm Of Ardga was developed as a solo Unity project to improve my understanding of AI programming, persistent player data, RPG systems, combat mechanics, and gameplay architecture while creating a complete gameplay experience.

What I Learned: Throughout development I gained practical experience building enemy AI, combat systems, experience and leveling mechanics, skill trees, persistent player progression, animation events, sprite sorting, gameplay architecture, and debugging interconnected gameplay systems.

Biggest Challenge: One of the largest challenges was implementing persistent player progression across multiple scenes while ensuring player level, unlocked skills, and collected coins remained synchronized throughout the game.

Gameplay Features

Combat and Enemy AI

Combat is driven by state-based enemy AI that allows enemies to detect the player, chase them, and attack once within range. Each enemy has unique attack ranges, attack speeds, and behaviours to create varied combat encounters. Player also must manage stamina to avoid overextending attacks and leaving themselves vulnerable to enemy counterattacks.

Implemented Features

  • Stamina-gated melee attacks.
  • Enemy detection system.
  • State-based AI (Idle, Chase, Attack).
  • Unique enemy attack ranges.
  • Health and damage system.
  • Player combat mechanics.
  • Loot drops from defeated enemies.

World Systems

The world combines exploration with RPG mechanics through shops, loot collection, enemy encounters, and intuitive UI systems. Sprite sorting and layered environments create believable depth throughout the game world.

Design Highlights

  • Shop system.
  • Loot collection.
  • HUD and gameplay UI.
  • Player health display.
  • Scene transitions.
  • 2D sprite sorting and layering.

Player Progression

Players earn experience by defeating enemies and leveling up to unlock new abilities. A three-branch skill tree allows players to specialise in different combat styles while persistent player progression carries progress across scenes.

Gameplay Systems

  • Experience and leveling system.
  • Three-branch skill tree.
  • Persistent player progression.
  • Character stat progression.
  • Unlockable combat abilities.
  • Progressive gameplay difficulty.

Technical Notes

Realm Of Ardga was built in Unity using C# and follows an object-oriented, modular architecture. Enemy behaviours are implemented using state-based AI, while gameplay systems communicate through event-driven logic. Persistent player data allows progression to carry seamlessly across multiple scenes.
